📸 A Few Snapshots

🌷 The Catalyst

My interest in cybersecurity started when my father’s bank account was hacked and he lost money. I was 19. That was the first time I saw technology stop being “just systems” and become something that can directly affect real people.

It wasn’t just curiosity—it was frustration, awareness, and responsibility all at once. That moment changed how I look at technology. Since then, I’ve been drawn toward understanding how systems fail, how they are attacked, and how they can be protected.
